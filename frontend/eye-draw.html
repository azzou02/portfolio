Stare

This colored pencil artwork juxtaposes a hyper- realistic eye with a surreal landscape, framed within a dark background resembling a chalkboard. The dark frame, scattered with phrases like "who I am" and "see what I'm seeing," creates the feeling of being observed while also suggesting introspection. This composition merges two environments - the internal world of perception symbolized by the eye and plant, and the external reality represented by the textured, message-filled surround. The piece invites viewers to contemplate the nature of observation, self-reflection, and the interplay between inner visions and outer realities, all while evoking a sense of being watched.
