Duck Composition

This is an oil painting that captures my appreciation for ducks and their natural habitats. The scene depicts a Hooded Merganser nesting among reeds and marsh grasses, with two eggs visible. I focused on creating a serene wetland atmosphere, using warm golden tones for the vegetation contrasted against cooler blues and purples for the water and sky. By creating this nesting scene, I wanted to highlight the beauty of wetland ecosystems and the cycle of life they support. The composition invites viewers to connect with the duck's world, emphasizing the importance of preserving these environments. Overall, this piece is both a personal expression of my love for ducks and a broader celebration of wildlife and natural beauty.
